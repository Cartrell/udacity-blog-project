Kimg Slime

Brief

King Slime is a pre-Hardmode boss.

His is an oversized blue slime, wearing a jeweled golden crown and having what appears to be a ninja inside of him.

As King Slime takes damage, he spawns additional ordinary Blue Slimes (and Spiked Slimes in Expert and Master Mode). He will also shrink in size the more damage he takes.

Fighting King Slime is not required for game progression, but defeating him early on rewards you with useful items.

Stats

Health: 2000 Damage: 40 Defense: 10

Spawning Conditions

King Slime spawns under one of the following conditions:

  • You use a Slime Crown.
  • One-hundred and fifty slimes are defeated during the Slime Rain event (or 75 if King Slime was already defeated at least once).
  • Randomly, rarely in the outer lateral sixths of the map, during daytime, and only on grass.

Notes and Tips

King Slime can teleport right on top of you, so watch out for that!

However, you can exploit this by making him teleport into a small area, causing him to get stuck for a little while.

The Blue Slimes that are spawned during the fight can drop Hearts and Stars, helping keep you in the fight if King Slime and company be puttin' a whoopin' on ya, haha!

According to Redigit (the creator of Terraria), the trapped ninja is the Guide's brother, and he was consumed by King Slime as a result of losing the fight against it. This statement was most likely a joke, and is not considered official lore.
