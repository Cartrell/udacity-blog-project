The Guide

Brief

The Guide is usually the first NPC you will encounter, as he spawns near you when the world is created.

He provides tips mainly aimed at new players, and displays crafting recipes. He knows about many items in this game. In that regard, he's kind of like the Deckard Kain of this game.

He defends himself using a wooden bow and arrow.

If he dies while in the Underworld via lava, or if a Guide Voodoo Doo is destroyed in the Underworld, it will summon the Wall of Flesh.

While he's not particularly useful (he neither sells anything, nor is he involved in quests), he does provide tips on game progression in addition to his vast knowledge of crafting recipes.

Spawning Conditions

Unlike most other NPCs, the Guide has no specific criteria since he automatically spawns at the game. However, if he is killed, he'll need a house before he will resawn.
