Forest

Brief

The Forest occupies much of world's surface, and is typically where you spawn at the start of the game.

Because it's the starting point, the monsters here are not powerful, and their spawn rates are not out of control (unlike some of the more difficult biomes).

It's mostly occupied by slims during the day, and zombies and flying eyeballs during night.

King Slime can spawn on the outer edges of the world in the forest, thogh this is rare.

The forest is plentiful with critters, such as bunnies, squirrels, birds, goldfish, butterflies, dragonflies, grasshoppers, turtles, worms, stinkbugs, and fireflies and owls at night.

You might find a cave in a dirt/grass hill that leads to the underground.
